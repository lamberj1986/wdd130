White Water Rafting Siteplan

Author: Jeff Lambert

Overview

Purpose

Have you ever wanted to go rafting, but have never had the chance to to do so? Have you ever thought that rafting costs too much for you to go? Well we have a plan in place that makes it easy to overcome both of these problems. We enjoy rafting so much here that we want to take everybody along for a ride, letting everybody share in our passion. Hopefully we will get to see you on the rapids soon!

Audience

Here at our rafting compnay we want to help those who have not had an opportunity to enjoy the rapids to get out and see what white water rafting is all about. Due to safety concerns, we do need to ensure that the youngest rafter that comes with us is at least 12 years old. For those rafters that are between the ages of 12 and 18, we do require that and has an adult rafting with them. We hope that you will join us in enjoying the thrill of going down the river and hitting all of the rapids that we can. We know that this may be the mising piece in your life, a thrill that you will always look back on and smile.

  • Why would you want to go white water rafting?
    • We know that there is a thirll being on the water heading down river and we want to share that with you.
  • Who are we targeting?
    • Any first time rafters that are 18 years or older, or for those first time rafters between the ages of 12-18 who ride with a responsible adult.
  • How can you access this site?
    • Hopefully we have everything in place that you can access this information and book a trip with us on any device that has internet access, whether that be your desktop, laptop, tablet, or smartphone.
