Our Top Tier Trips

Our premier trip, take your time to acclimate to the Mountains on this 7 day/6 night trip. Our gides will take you through all the different class rapids found on the river. Each night you will camp on the river banks and enjoy the beautiful night sky in Colordo.

Get to know the Cache la Poudre river and learn why Northern Colorado loves this natural resource. Over the course of 3 days on the river you will get to experience all that this river has to offer. Learn how to cook dutch oven meals each night and see what it is like to be an early explorer.

If you are looking for a longer advendure without hitting all of the Class V rapids, this trip is for you. Get to know what it is like to traverse a river on this trip. You will get to travel over 150 miles down the Yampa River seeing all of the great spots that locals love.

Get to know what it is like to white water raft on this overnight trip. You will get your feet wet and get a taste for adventure. This trip will have you sleeping on the river bank for a night, and learning what it is like to rough it in the Colorado back country.
