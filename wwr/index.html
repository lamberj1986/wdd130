We here at Splish Splash are all about making sure that you have the most fun on your trip down the river.

If you go home dry then you didn't fully experience Splish Splash Rafting!



We here at Splish Splash Rafting want to help everybody experience what white water rafting is all about. Hopefully we will create a lifelong passion for riding the rapids.

We offer a variety of different rafting experiences. We have trips catered to beginners all the way up to week long expiditions that hit the most advanced class rapids that we can legally take you on.
